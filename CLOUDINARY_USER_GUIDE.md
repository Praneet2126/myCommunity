# User Guide: Profile Photo Upload

## How to Upload Your Profile Photo

### Step 1: Navigate to Your Profile

1. Log in to your myCommunity account
2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner
3. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
4. You'll be taken to `/profile` page

### Step 2: Upload Your Photo

1. **Hover over your current profile photo**
   - You'll see a dark overlay appear
   - A camera icon and "Change Photo" text will appear

2. **Click on the photo**
   - A file picker dialog will open

3. **Select your image**
   - Choose a photo from your computer
   - Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP
   - Maximum size: 5MB

4. **Wait for upload**
   - A loading spinner will appear
   - The upload typically takes 1-3 seconds
   - Your photo will be automatically optimized

5. **Success!**
   - You'll see a success message
   - Your new photo will appear immediately
   - The photo is now stored securely in the cloud

### Step 3: Update Other Profile Information (Optional)

While on the profile page, you can also update:
- **Full Name**: Your display name
- **Phone Number**: Your contact number (E.164 format)

Click "Save Changes" to update these fields.

## Troubleshooting

### "No file uploaded" error
- Make sure you selected a file
- Try selecting the file again

### "Only image files are allowed" error
- You tried to upload a non-image file
- Use JPG, PNG, GIF, or WEBP format only

### "File size must be less than 5MB" error
- Your image is too large
- Compress the image before uploading
- Use online tools like TinyPNG or Squoosh

### "Authentication required" error
- Your session may have expired
- Log out and log back in
- Try uploading again

### Photo not updating
- Refresh the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Check your internet connection

## Privacy & Security

### Your Photo is Secure
- Stored on Cloudinary's secure servers
- Delivered via encrypted CDN
- Only accessible via your profile
- Old photos are automatically deleted

### Who Can See Your Photo?
- Other users in your cities
- Members of your chat groups
- Anyone viewing your profile

### Deleting Your Photo
To remove your profile photo:
1. Upload a new photo to replace it
2. Or contact support to reset to default

1. **Use Good Lighting**
   - Natural light works best
   - Avoid harsh shadows

2. **Center Your Face**
   - The photo will be cropped to a circle
   - Keep your face in the center

3. **Use Recent Photos**
   - Help others recognize you
   - Update periodically

4. **Keep It Professional**
   - This is a community platform
   - Choose appropriate images

5. **Optimize Before Upload**
   - Compress large images
   - Crop to square if possible
   - Remove any personal information from metadata

## Technical Details

### What Happens When You Upload?

1. **Selection**: You choose a file from your device
2. **Validation**: The app checks file type and size
3. **Preview**: You see a preview of your photo
4. **Upload**: File is sent to the server
5. **Processing**: Cloudinary optimizes and resizes
6. **Storage**: Photo is stored in the cloud
7. **Update**: Your profile is updated with the new URL
8. **Cleanup**: Old photo is deleted (if exists)
9. **Display**: New photo appears everywhere in the app

### Image Transformations Applied

- **Resize**: Maximum 500x500 pixels
- **Crop**: Maintains aspect ratio
- **Quality**: Auto-optimized for web
- **Format**: Converted to optimal format
- **CDN**: Delivered via global CDN for fast loading

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upload multiple photos?
Currently, only one profile photo is supported. You can change it anytime.

### How long does the upload take?
Typically 1-3 seconds, depending on your internet speed and file size.

### Will my photo be compressed?
Yes, photos are automatically optimized for web display while maintaining quality.

### Can I use the same photo on multiple accounts?
Yes, but each account stores its own copy.

### What if I don't upload a photo?
A default placeholder image will be used.

### Can I revert to a previous photo?
No, old photos are deleted when you upload a new one. Save copies locally if needed.

### Is there a limit to how many times I can change my photo?
No limit, but please use the feature responsibly.

### What happens if my upload fails?
- Your current photo remains unchanged
- You'll see an error message
- Try again or contact support
