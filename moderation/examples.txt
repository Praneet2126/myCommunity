# Content Moderation Test Examples
# Format: EXPECTED_RESULT|MESSAGE_TEXT
# EXPECTED_RESULT can be: BLOCK, FLAG, or ALLOW
# Lines starting with # are comments and will be ignored

# Spam examples
BLOCK|BUY NOW!!! CLICK HERE!!! SPECIAL DEAL!!!
BLOCK|aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaa
BLOCK|Check out this amazing deal!!!!! Only $99!!!!!
FLAG|This is a great hotel with amazing reviews!!!

# Promotion examples
BLOCK|DM me for the best hotel deals! Contact me now!
BLOCK|Limited time offer! 50% off! Act now! Call 123-456-7890
FLAG|I found a good deal on Booking.com, you should check it out
ALLOW|I stayed at the Marriott and it was great

# Suspicious links
BLOCK|Check this out: http://192.168.1.1/offer
BLOCK|Visit bit.ly/xyz123 for amazing deals
FLAG|Here's a link: https://example.com
ALLOW|I recommend checking out booking.com for hotels

# Toxic content (will need AI)
BLOCK|You are an idiot
FLAG|That's a stupid idea
ALLOW|I disagree with your opinion

# Normal messages
ALLOW|Hello, how are you doing?
ALLOW|Has anyone been to Paris? I'm planning a trip
ALLOW|The weather in Mumbai is great today!
ALLOW|Thanks for the recommendation!

# Edge cases
ALLOW|I love traveling to NYC!
ALLOW|The hotel was $200 per night
FLAG|FREE SHIPPING! BUY NOW! LIMITED TIME!
BLOCK|spam spam spam spam spam

# Travel-specific legitimate content
ALLOW|I'm planning a trip to Goa next month
ALLOW|The beaches in Maldives are beautiful
ALLOW|Has anyone tried the street food in Bangkok?
ALLOW|I recommend visiting the Taj Mahal at sunrise

# More spam patterns
BLOCK|!!!!!URGENT!!!!! CLICK NOW!!!!!
BLOCK|$$$$$ MAKE MONEY FAST $$$$$
FLAG|Check out my travel blog at example.com
ALLOW|I found a great restaurant recommendation

# Link variations
BLOCK|Visit http://suspicious-site.tk/offer
FLAG|Check this: https://bit.ly/abc123
ALLOW|Here's the official site: https://www.booking.com
ALLOW|I booked through Expedia and it was fine
